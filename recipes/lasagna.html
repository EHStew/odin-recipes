Lasagna

Description

A lasagna that only takes 90 minutes to prepare, and can serve up to 8! A family recipe passed down from my grandmother to me, this lasagna is sure to put a smile on everyones faces.

Ingredients

  • 1(16 Ounce) package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1(16 ounce) Jar of Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of ricotta Cheese
